AUTO TURNER

This project is the work of three makerspace students from the Summer Stem 2018 program at Cooper Union: Samson Zhang, Rebecca Kanter, and Abigahile Bruny.

Who is our product for?

Our product is for all musicians who have trouble balancing playing their music and flipping the pages. All ages and genders can use this product.
This product can be used in orchestras, music classrooms, and for solo practice and performance.

What problem does our product solve?

People who have to play instruments are not able to also flip their music with their hands at the same time. Hiring a professional page turner to take care of this is both costly and impractical for everyday use.

What is our solution?

Our solution is the page turner which can flip individual sheets with the touch of a button.
This product sits on a music stand and uses wheels to lift the paper up.
It also includes storage area for all your storage needs.
